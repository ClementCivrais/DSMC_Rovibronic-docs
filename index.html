DSMC Rovibronic

A DSMC (Direct Simulation Monte Carlo) solver for hypersonic non-equilibrium gas flows, built on top of OpenFOAM v2512 and extended with a state-to-state rovibronic description of molecular internal energy — individual electronic, vibrational, and rotational quantum states (\(e_{qn}, v_{qn}, j_{qn}\)) are tracked on each simulator particle.

The solver targets shock-heated air, CO2, and N2 flows where thermal non-equilibrium and chemistry are tightly coupled and a multi-temperature description is inadequate.
