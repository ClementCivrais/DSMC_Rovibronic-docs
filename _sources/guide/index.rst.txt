User and Developer Guide
========================

The present guide combines user-facing and developer-facing
material in a single, continuous reading order. We begin by
installing the full tool-chain —  from OpenFOAM itself through to
the rovibronic extension —  run the reference case, and work our
way down into the dictionaries, the post-processing workflow, the
source-tree architecture, and the conventions to follow when
adding a new species, reaction, or relaxation sub-model. The guide
closes with the planned regression-test framework, which we intend
to tie into every rebuild of the solver.
