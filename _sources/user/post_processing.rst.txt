Post-processing
===============

The solver writes the usual OpenFOAM time directories plus
per-particle fields capturing the full rovibronic state.

Macroscopic fields
------------------

Standard DSMC volume fields — number density, translational and
internal temperatures, pressure, and species mass fractions — are
written to ``<case>/<time>/`` and can be visualised with
``paraFoam``.

Quantum-state populations
-------------------------

Particle-level quantum numbers (:math:`e_{qn}, v_{qn}, j_{qn}`)
are stored in the Lagrangian cloud. Dedicated utilities under
``applications/utilities/`` extract state populations and build
Boltzmann / non-Boltzmann distribution diagnostics.

Validation diagnostics
----------------------

The :doc:`../reference/validation` page collects canonical checks
(heat-bath relaxation, isothermal dissociation, shock profiles) used
to confirm the rovibronic machinery against analytical and published
results.
