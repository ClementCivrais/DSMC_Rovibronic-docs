DSMC Rovibronic
===============

A DSMC (Direct Simulation Monte Carlo) solver for hypersonic
non-equilibrium gas flows, built on top of OpenFOAM v2512 and
extended with a state-to-state **rovibronic** description of molecular
internal energy — individual electronic, vibrational, and rotational
quantum states (:math:`e_{qn}, v_{qn}, j_{qn}`) are tracked on each
simulator particle.

The solver targets shock-heated air, CO\ :sub:`2`\ , and N\ :sub:`2`
flows where thermal non-equilibrium and chemistry are tightly coupled
and a multi-temperature description is inadequate.
