Rovibronic Level Structure
==========================

The molecular internal energy is expanded as

.. math::

   E_{\text{int}}(e, v, j) = E_e(e) + E_v(e, v) + E_r(e, v, j),

where :math:`E_e` is the electronic term energy, :math:`E_v` is the
vibrational term in electronic level :math:`e`, and :math:`E_r` is
the rotational term in the :math:`(e, v)` manifold. Each contribution
is obtained from a Dunham expansion,

.. math::

   E_v(e, v) = \sum_k Y_{k,0}(e)\,(v + 1/2)^k,

.. math::

   E_r(e, v, j) = \sum_{k,\ell} Y_{k,\ell}(e)\,(v + 1/2)^k\,[j(j+1)]^\ell,

fitted to ab-initio potential-energy surfaces. See
``docs/Database/dunham_coefficients_from_ab_initio_PES.md`` for the
fit procedure and quality metrics.

Level enumeration and cut-offs
------------------------------

* Dissociation cut-off: maximum bound :math:`v` for each electronic
  level is set where the fitted Dunham series diverges from the
  physical PES.
* Rotational cut-off: :math:`j_{\max}(e, v)` is enforced from a
  centrifugal-barrier criterion.
* Electronic levels: populated up to the highest state included in
  the species database.

Degeneracies
------------

Nuclear spin, electronic angular momentum, and rotational degeneracy
:math:`(2j + 1)` are carried per level so that equilibrium (Boltzmann)
populations are reproduced exactly in the absence of departures from
equilibrium.
