Theory guide
============

The present guide gives a comprehensive account of the physics and
numerics implemented in the solver, with the goal of mapping every
capability of the code to its mathematical foundation. Each page
may be read as a stand-alone technical note and includes
derivations at a level of detail sufficient to verify, modify, or
extend the corresponding implementation.
