Chemistry
=========

Chemistry is handled as state-specific or state-aggregated reactions
sampled during the collision phase.

Reaction families
-----------------

* **Dissociation** :math:`\mathrm{AB}(e, v, j) + \mathrm{M} \to
  \mathrm{A} + \mathrm{B} + \mathrm{M}`
* **Recombination** (inverse of dissociation) — two-body and, where
  enabled, three-body (``recombinePQ``).
* **Exchange** :math:`\mathrm{AB} + \mathrm{C} \to \mathrm{AC} +
  \mathrm{B}`
* **Electron-impact ionisation / excitation** (where species
  database includes the relevant channels).

Rate models
-----------

Three chemistry backends are implemented:

* **TCE** (Total Collision Energy) — Arrhenius-like from collision
  energy, the stock DSMC model.
* **QK** (Quantum-Kinetic) — Bird's threshold-based model extended
  to per-state activation energies.
* **State-specific** — per-:math:`(e, v, j)` dissociation rates read
  from the species database.

The choice is driven by ``chemistrySubmodel`` in
``constant/dsmcProperties``.

Post-reaction sampling
----------------------

Internal energy of reaction products is sampled to preserve
detailed balance against equilibrium populations. A report on
bug-audit fixes affecting post-reaction sampling is kept at
``docs/Rovibronic Article/DSMC_Bugs_Report.pdf``.
