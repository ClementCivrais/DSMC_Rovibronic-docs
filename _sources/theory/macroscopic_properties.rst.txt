Macroscopic Properties
======================

Volume-averaged fields are accumulated from simulator particles in
each cell.

Number density and species fractions
------------------------------------

.. math::

   n_s = \frac{N_s w}{V_{\text{cell}}}, \qquad
   x_s = \frac{n_s}{\sum_{s'} n_{s'}},

where :math:`N_s` is the number of particles of species :math:`s` in
the cell and :math:`w` is the particle weight.

Temperatures
------------

* **Translational** :math:`T_t` — from the peculiar-velocity second
  moment.
* **Rotational** :math:`T_r` — from the rotational-energy per molecule
  under the quantum rotational partition function.
* **Vibrational** :math:`T_v` — same, using the vibrational partition
  function of each electronic level.
* **Electronic** :math:`T_e` — from the distribution across
  electronic levels.

In the state-to-state regime these "temperatures" are best
interpreted as effective temperatures extracted from the non-Boltzmann
populations; the physical solution lives in the population vector
itself.

Population distributions
------------------------

Per-cell histograms over :math:`(e, v, j)` are available via the
post-processing utilities (see :doc:`../user/post_processing`).
