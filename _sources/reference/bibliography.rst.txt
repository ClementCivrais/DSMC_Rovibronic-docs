Bibliography
============

The core references used in building and validating the rovibronic
DSMC solver. Copies live under ``docs/References/`` in the source
tree.

Primary references
------------------

* G. A. Bird — *Molecular Gas Dynamics and the Direct Simulation of
  Gas Flows*, Oxford (1994).
* C. White — *Benchmarking, Development and Applications of an Open
  Source DSMC Solver*, PhD thesis.
* M. Pfeiffer — *Polyatomic extensions to DSMC*.
* M. Pfeiffer — *VSS collision cross-section model*.
* L. Babou — *Partition functions for diatomic molecules*.
* Bird / Chou / Gao — *Post-reaction internal energy redistribution*.

Solver-specific notes
---------------------

The following in-repo documents capture derivations specific to this
solver:

* ``docs/Rovibronic Article/Article_Draft_Rovibronic_DSMC.pdf`` —
  method derivation.
* ``docs/Source Code Report/rovibronic_dsmc_report.pdf`` — solver
  implementation walkthrough.
* ``doc/LT_Correction_MultiMode.pdf`` — multi-mode
  Landau–Teller correction.
* ``doc/LandauTeller_MultiExponential_Derivation.pdf`` —
  multi-exponential Landau–Teller relaxation time.
* ``doc/CoupledRelaxationMatrix_Derivation.pdf`` — coupled
  vibration–rotation relaxation operator.
* ``doc/chemistry_comparison_piclas_mnf.pdf`` — chemistry
  benchmark against PICLas.
* ``doc/bug_audit.pdf`` — internal bug audit.
