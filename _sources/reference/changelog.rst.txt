Changelog
=========

Notable changes to the solver, newest first.

Unreleased
----------

* Online documentation scaffolded and re-drafted in the lead
  developer's voice, with theory, input reference, examples, and
  a regression-test roadmap.
* Landau-Teller multi-mode enforcement (parallel-marginal cascade)
  landed; phase-by-phase validation in progress.

Recent
------

* Database update — Dunham fits to ab-initio potential-energy
  surfaces.
* Restructuration of the code and bug fixes, covered in the draft
  bug-audit report at
  ``docs/Rovibronic Article/DSMC_Bugs_Report.pdf``.
* Improved database, fixed chemical reactions, added species.
* Correction of measurements and boundary conditions; addition of
  the in-repo LaTeX reports under ``doc/``.
* Port of the full rovibronic :math:`(n, v, J)` quantum-state
  tracking onto the dsmcFoam+ framework.
