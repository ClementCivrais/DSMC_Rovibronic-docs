Validation
==========

Validation status of the rovibronic machinery.

Heat-bath relaxation
--------------------

* Multi-mode Landau–Teller enforcement verified: the
  :math:`Z_{\text{fit}}/Z_{\text{input}}` ratio sits at
  :math:`\approx 0.99` without the ZCorr correction, confirming
  that ZCorr is **not** needed on the rovibronic Larsen–Borgnakke
  path.

2-T recombination & three-body channel
--------------------------------------

* 2-T recombination and the three-body ``recombinePQ`` channel have
  been exercised in 0-D and 1-D canonical cases.
* A Larsen–Borgnakke NaN failure mode, traced to unbounded inelastic
  energy sampling under specific reaction conditions, has been
  isolated and fixed.
* A dissociation double-count bug was identified and fixed
  (details in ``docs/Rovibronic Article/DSMC_Bugs_Report.pdf``).

Shock profiles
--------------

* 1-D normal-shock profile benchmarks against published Park /
  Macheret results are tracked under ``run/`` as separate test
  cases.

Outstanding items
-----------------

* Full 3-D blunt-body validation (pending).
* Electronic-state excitation benchmarks against state-resolved
  master-equation solutions (pending).
