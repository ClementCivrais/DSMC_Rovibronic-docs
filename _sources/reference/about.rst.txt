About
=====

DSMC Rovibronic is a state-to-state extension of the OpenFOAM DSMC
library developed by Clement Civrais, targeting hypersonic
non-equilibrium flows where a multi-temperature description is
inadequate.

The solver builds on top of the MNF (Molecular Nonequilibrium Flow)
branch of OpenFOAM v2512 and ports the full rovibronic
:math:`(e_{qn}, v_{qn}, j_{qn})` quantum-state tracking into that
framework.

Scope
-----

* DSMC in the rarefied and transitional regimes.
* Molecular species: N\ :sub:`2`, O\ :sub:`2`, NO, CO, CO\ :sub:`2`,
  and their dissociation / exchange products.
* Hypersonic shock-layer chemistry with explicit electronic-state
  populations.
