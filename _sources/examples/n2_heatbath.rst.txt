N\ :sub:`2` Heat-Bath Relaxation
================================

A zero-dimensional (0-D) isothermal heat-bath relaxation is the
cheapest end-to-end validation of the rovibronic relaxation
machinery. The gas is initialised at a cold internal temperature
while the translational temperature is held high, and the approach
to equilibrium is compared to the Landau–Teller analytical solution.

Case layout
-----------

.. code-block:: text

   run/<case>/
   ├── 0/              # initial fields
   ├── constant/       # species, dsmcProperties, rovibronic settings
   ├── system/         # blockMeshDict, controlDict, decomposeParDict
   └── postProcessing/ # populated at runtime

Running
-------

.. code-block:: bash

   cd run/<case>
   blockMesh && dsmcInitialise && dsmcFoamRovibronic

Expected result
---------------

* :math:`T_v(t)` relaxes toward :math:`T_t` on the Millikan–White
  timescale.
* Final state populations match the Boltzmann distribution at
  :math:`T_t` within statistical noise.
* The :math:`Z_{\text{fit}}/Z_{\text{input}}` ratio should sit
  within :math:`\approx 1\%` of unity — confirming the multi-mode LT
  enforcement is unbiased.
