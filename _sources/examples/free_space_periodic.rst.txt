Free-space periodic equilibrium
===============================

Directory: ``tutorials/freeSpacePeriodic/``.

A periodic cube of N\ :sub:`2` / O\ :sub:`2` in thermodynamic
equilibrium. The case has no walls, no body forces, no
chemistry; the gas is initialised at the target state and the
solver is run for long enough to confirm that the sampled
statistics (temperature, density, stress tensor, mean free path)
remain within statistical bounds of the initial state.

This is the simplest possible baseline case and is useful as:

* a **smoke test** of the build — the case must run to
  completion without NaN or unphysical drift;
* a **convergence test** for the time-averaging machinery — the
  per-cell error estimate produced by ``measureErrors`` on
  ``dsmcVolFields`` should follow the :math:`1/\sqrt{N}`
  statistical convergence expected of a Maxwellian equilibrium;
* a **reference** for any chemistry-on case run with otherwise
  identical settings, so that the chemistry's effect on the
  macroscopic state can be isolated by difference.

Case parameters
---------------

* Species: N\ :sub:`2`, O\ :sub:`2`.
* Fully periodic cube.
* Run-time configured in ``system/controlDict``; the default
  end-time is sufficient to confirm that the sampled macroscopic
  fields have settled.

.. code-block:: bash

   cd tutorials/freeSpacePeriodic
   blockMesh
   dsmcInitialise
   dsmcFoam
