Examples
========

The solver ships with a suite of ten reference tutorials under
``tutorials/`` in the source tree, covering the principal classes
of DSMC simulation targeted by the rovibronic extension: from
zero-dimensional heat-bath validation through micro-channel
flows, external hypersonic bodies, and a representative re-entry
capsule.

Every tutorial shares the same three-fold OpenFOAM layout
(``0/`` field directory, ``constant/`` with ``dsmcProperties``
and ``speciesCache/``, ``system/`` with the run-time
dictionaries) and runs to completion through the standard
``blockMesh`` → ``dsmcInitialise`` → ``dsmcFoam`` pipeline.
