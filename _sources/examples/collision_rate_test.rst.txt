Collision-rate test
===================

Directory: ``tutorials/collisionRateTest/``.

A periodic N\ :sub:`2` / O\ :sub:`2` case specifically designed
to stress the collision-partner-selection layer. The
``cellMeasurements`` class accumulates the per-cell collision
count and the mean collision separation, both of which are
written alongside the macroscopic fields. The case allows a
direct comparison between the theoretical collision frequency

.. math::

   \nu_{\mathrm{coll}}
     = \frac{1}{2}\,n\,\langle\sigma_T\,g\rangle

evaluated from the VSS parameters of the species and the
collision rate sampled by the solver.

Agreement to within statistical noise is the sanity check that
the No-Time-Counter estimator
(:doc:`../theory/collision_sampling`) is correctly implemented
and that the acceptance probability
:math:`\sigma_T g / (\sigma_T g)_{\max}` is not systematically
biased.

Running
-------

.. code-block:: bash

   cd tutorials/collisionRateTest
   blockMesh
   dsmcInitialise
   dsmcFoam
