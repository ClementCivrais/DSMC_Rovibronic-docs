Micro-scale channel flow
========================

Directory: ``tutorials/microScaleTestCase/``.

A pressure-driven micro-channel flow of N\ :sub:`2` in the
transitional regime. The case exercises the pressure-inlet /
deletion-outlet combination on a rectangular channel geometry
and is designed to reproduce the measurements of Liou & Fang
[LiouFang2006mc]_ on mass-flow rate versus Knudsen number, which
served as one of the original benchmarks of the dsmcFoam+ solver
[White2013mc]_.

Because the channel height sits at
:math:`\mathrm{Kn} \sim 0.2`, the flow is firmly in the regime
where DSMC is the method of choice (see
:doc:`../theory/foundations`), and neither a pure continuum nor a
pure free-molecular description would be adequate.

Diagnostics
-----------

* The ``dsmcBinsMethod`` field model in
  ``system/fieldPropertiesDict`` samples a 1-D bin stack along
  the channel axis; each bin returns translational temperature,
  density, and mean velocity.
* A ``dsmcMassFluxSurface`` records the mass flow rate through
  the inlet and outlet patches for direct comparison against
  [LiouFang2006mc]_.

Running
-------

.. code-block:: bash

   cd tutorials/microScaleTestCase
   blockMesh
   dsmcInitialise
   dsmcFoam

.. [LiouFang2006mc] W. W. Liou and Y. Fang,
   *Microfluid Mechanics: Principles and Modeling*, McGraw-Hill
   (2006).
.. [White2013mc] C. White, *Benchmarking, Development and
   Applications of an Open Source DSMC Solver*, PhD thesis,
   University of Glasgow (2013).
