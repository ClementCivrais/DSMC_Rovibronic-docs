Landau-Teller heat-bath relaxation
==================================

Directory: ``tutorials/relaxationLandauTeller/``.

A zero-dimensional (0-D) isothermal heat-bath case that exercises
the parallel-marginal relaxation cascade of the rovibronic
Larsen-Borgnakke sampler (:doc:`../theory/relaxation`). The
translational temperature is held at :math:`T_t = 10\,000~\mathrm{K}`
by the large thermal inertia of the heat bath, and the internal
state is initialised at :math:`T_\mathrm{int}(0) = 1\,000~\mathrm{K}`.
The mean internal energy is expected to decay exponentially toward
its equilibrium value at the heat-bath temperature.

Phase selection
---------------

The tutorial is configured for **Phase 1** of the four-phase
validation protocol documented in :doc:`../reference/validation`:
only the rotational channel is active, the rovibrational and
rovibronic channels are silenced by setting their collision
numbers to :math:`10^{9}`. To run the remaining phases, copy the
case and adjust the three collision-number entries in
``constant/dsmcProperties``:

.. code-block:: text

   Phase 1 (rotational only):     Z_r = 5,    Z_rv = 1e9, Z_rve = 1e9
   Phase 2 (rovibrational):       Z_r = 1e9,  Z_rv = 50,  Z_rve = 1e9
   Phase 3 (rovibronic, spin-cons.):      Z_r = 5,  Z_rv = 50,  Z_rve = 500   (allowSpinForbidden=false)
   Phase 4 (rovibronic, full manifold):   Z_r = 5,  Z_rv = 50,  Z_rve = 500   (allowSpinForbidden=true)

Case parameters
---------------

* Species: O\ :sub:`2`.
* Number density: :math:`10^{22}\;\mathrm{m}^{-3}`, so that the
  heat bath contains enough parcels per cell for statistical
  convergence on a single-cell mesh.
* Translational temperature: :math:`10\,000~\mathrm{K}`.
* Internal temperature at :math:`t = 0`:
  :math:`1\,000~\mathrm{K}`.
* Mesh: :math:`1 \times 1 \times 1` cell of side
  :math:`1\;\mu\mathrm{m}`, fully periodic.
* Time-step: :math:`10^{-11}~\mathrm{s}`, total integration
  :math:`5 \times 10^{-7}~\mathrm{s}`.

Running the case
----------------

.. code-block:: bash

   cd tutorials/relaxationLandauTeller
   blockMesh
   dsmcInitialise
   dsmcFoam

Post-processing
---------------

The zone diagnostic in ``system/fieldPropertiesDict`` writes a
time history of the rotational, vibrational, and electronic
effective temperatures to ``fieldMeasurements/``. Plot the
relaxing mode's energy against

.. math::

   \frac{\langle E_k\rangle^{\star} - \langle E_k\rangle(t)}
        {\langle E_k\rangle^{\star} - \langle E_k\rangle(0)}
   = \exp\!\left(-\frac{t}{Z_k^{\mathrm{cont}}\,\tau_c}\right),

and extract the fitted :math:`Z_k` from the decay time-constant.
The Phase 1 run should recover the input :math:`Z_r` to within
5%.

Expected results
----------------

The four phases of the validation protocol produce the reference
curves shown below. Each figure overlays the DSMC relaxation
(markers) on the analytical Landau-Teller solution (continuous
line) restricted to the state-space actually reachable by the
sampler in that phase. A summary figure combining Phases 1–3 is
shown first.

.. figure:: ../_static/figures/landau_teller/phase123_combined.png
   :align: center
   :width: 90%
   :alt: DSMC rovibronic relaxation versus Landau-Teller reference,
         Phases 1, 2, and 3.

   *Combined overview* —  DSMC relaxation compared to the
   analytical Landau-Teller solution for Phases 1 (rotational
   only), 2 (rovibrational), and 3 (rovibronic, spin-conserving).
   The DSMC points sit on the analytical curves to within
   statistical noise for every phase.

Phase 1 — rotational only
`````````````````````````

Only the rotational channel is active
(:math:`Z_r = 5`, :math:`Z_{rv} = Z_{rve} = 10^{6}`). The mean
rotational energy per parcel decays exponentially toward
:math:`\langle E_R\rangle^{\star}` at the heat-bath temperature.
The DSMC markers sit on the analytical single-exponential LT
curve to within a fraction of a percent.

.. figure:: ../_static/figures/landau_teller/phase1.png
   :align: center
   :width: 80%
   :alt: Phase 1 — rotational-only relaxation, DSMC vs LT.

   *Phase 1.* Rotational relaxation at :math:`Z_r = 5`; DSMC on
   the analytical Landau-Teller curve. The reference
   :math:`E_R^{\star}` is the J-Boltzmann at the bath
   temperature, evaluated on the rotational ladder of the
   reactant :math:`(n_p, v_p)`.

Phase 2 — rovibrational
```````````````````````

The rovibrational channel is active
(:math:`Z_{rv} = 50`, :math:`Z_r = Z_{rve} = 10^{6}`). The
mean :math:`(v, J)` energy approaches equilibrium on a slower
time-scale than Phase 1, and again tracks the analytical LT
curve.

.. figure:: ../_static/figures/landau_teller/phase2.png
   :align: center
   :width: 80%
   :alt: Phase 2 — rovibrational relaxation, DSMC vs LT.

   *Phase 2.* Rovibrational relaxation at :math:`Z_{rv} = 50`.
   The reference :math:`E_{RV}^{\star}` is the
   :math:`(v, J)`-Boltzmann at the bath temperature, evaluated
   on the rovibrational sub-manifold of the parcel's electronic
   state :math:`n_p`.

Phase 3 — rovibronic, spin-conserving
`````````````````````````````````````

The rovibronic channel is active in its spin-conserving
variant (:math:`Z_{rve} = 500`, build flag
``allowSpinForbidden=false``). Only the triplet manifold is
sampled; the relaxation is single-exponential to leading order
but shows a small mid-decay residual relative to the single-
:math:`\tau` LT curve, which is the bi-exponential physics
signature of fast :math:`(v, J)` equilibration inside
:math:`n = 0` followed by slow access to the higher triplet
states.

.. figure:: ../_static/figures/landau_teller/phase3.png
   :align: center
   :width: 80%
   :alt: Phase 3 — spin-conserving rovibronic relaxation.

   *Phase 3.* Rovibronic relaxation at :math:`Z_{rve} = 500`,
   spin-conserving. The reference :math:`E_{RVE}^{\star}` is
   the triplet-only Boltzmann at the bath temperature.

Phase 4 — rovibronic, full manifold
```````````````````````````````````

The rovibronic channel is active with all electronic states
accessible (``allowSpinForbidden=true``). The low-lying
singlets of O\ :sub:`2` (a\ :sup:`1`\ Δ\ :sub:`g` at
:math:`\sim\!11\,400~\mathrm{K}` and b\ :sup:`1`\ Σ\ :sub:`g`\
:sup:`+` at :math:`\sim\!19\,000~\mathrm{K}`) equilibrate on
the same timescale as :math:`(v, J)` inside :math:`n = 0`, so
the relaxation is a clean single exponential.

.. figure:: ../_static/figures/landau_teller/phase4.png
   :align: center
   :width: 80%
   :alt: Phase 4 — full-manifold rovibronic relaxation.

   *Phase 4.* Rovibronic relaxation at :math:`Z_{rve} = 500`,
   full manifold. DSMC tracks the full :math:`(n, v, J)`
   Boltzmann reference on a single exponential.

These four figures are the canonical *acceptance reference* for
the relaxation module; any change to the Larsen-Borgnakke
sampler, to the Nizenkov correction tables, or to the
rovibronic database should be re-checked against them before
being committed.
