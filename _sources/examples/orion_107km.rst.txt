Orion capsule at 107 km (non-reacting)
======================================

Directory: ``tutorials/orion107kmNonReactingFlow/``.

Flow over the Orion crew capsule forebody at 107 km altitude
during re-entry, with chemistry disabled. The case is
representative of the upper-atmosphere portion of a lunar-return
trajectory, where the Knudsen number is large enough that DSMC
is essential (a continuum description fails) and the number
density is small enough that ionisation is not yet significant.

Theoretical basis
-----------------

The case is the canonical "DSMC is the right tool" scenario:

* :doc:`../theory/foundations` —  at 107 km the free-stream
  number density is :math:`\sim 10^{16}\;\mathrm{m}^{-3}` and
  the mean free path is of order metres; with a capsule
  characteristic length :math:`\sim\!5~\mathrm{m}`, the
  free-stream Knudsen number is :math:`\mathrm{Kn} \sim
  10^{-1}`. This sits in the upper transitional regime where
  Navier-Stokes with slip conditions is marginal at best, and
  DSMC is the only method that reliably captures the flow.
  The :doc:`../tools/mean_free_path` calculator is useful here
  for sanity-checking the free-stream mean free path and the
  cell size :math:`\Delta x < \lambda/3` required by the DSMC
  convergence condition.
* :doc:`../theory/wall_models` —  the capsule surface is
  modelled as a **fully-diffuse thermal wall**
  (``dsmcDiffuseWallPatch``) at :math:`T_w`; the resulting
  heat flux and drag are the engineering quantities of
  interest.
* :doc:`../theory/collision_cross_sections` —  at high altitude
  the collision frequency is low and the post-shock
  non-equilibrium region extends far back from the nose; the
  Landau-Teller cascade of :doc:`../theory/relaxation` governs
  the translational-rotational-vibrational equilibration in
  that region.

Chemistry is intentionally disabled to isolate the inviscid /
rarefied gas-dynamic contribution to drag and heat flux; a
reactive extension is left as a follow-up (the directory
contains an alternate ``chemReactDictReacting`` for that
purpose).

Validation goals
----------------

Three quantitative targets:

#. **Stagnation-point heat flux.** The integrated wall heat
   flux at the forebody stagnation point, built from the
   ``internalEBF_`` and kinetic-energy accumulators of
   :doc:`../classes/measurements`, is the key engineering
   output. The Fay-Riddell correlation provides a continuum
   reference; the DSMC value should be smaller than the
   Fay-Riddell estimate due to the finite-Knudsen-number
   attenuation.
#. **Integrated forebody drag.** The axial component of the
   integrated wall-momentum accumulator gives the drag
   coefficient :math:`C_D`, directly comparable to the
   Apollo Command Module and Orion flight-test databases.
#. **Shock stand-off distance.** The bow-shock stand-off at
   the stagnation line should agree with the finite-thickness
   analogue of the Ambrosio-Wortman correlation once the local
   density and Mach number are fed in.

Case parameters
---------------

* Species: N\ :sub:`2` and O\ :sub:`2`.
* Altitude: 107 km — representative free-stream density
  :math:`\sim 10^{16}\;\mathrm{m}^{-3}`; use the
  :doc:`../tools/mean_free_path` calculator to confirm the mean
  free path and Knudsen number at the case's reference length.
* Triangulated capsule geometry: ``constant/triSurface/``.
* Free-stream velocity and temperature in
  ``system/boundariesDict``.
* Walls: diffuse, at the specified :math:`T_w`.

Diagnostics
-----------

* Volume fields (density, translational and internal
  temperatures, stress tensor, heat flux, Mach number) via
  ``dsmcVolFields``.
* Wall heat flux and friction drag via
  ``boundaryMeasurements``; the integrated drag over the
  forebody is directly comparable to the Apollo Command Module
  and Orion flight data referenced in the literature.

Running
-------

.. code-block:: bash

   cd tutorials/orion107kmNonReactingFlow
   # Mesh generation — use the usual OpenFOAM pipeline appropriate
   # for the triangulated surface:
   blockMesh
   surfaceFeatureExtract   # if used
   snappyHexMesh -overwrite
   dsmcInitialise
   dsmcFoam
