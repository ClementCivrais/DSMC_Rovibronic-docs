Controllers
===========

Source: ``src/lagrangian/DSMC/controllers/``.

Role
----

Controllers are run-time agents that monitor a selected quantity
—  a cell-zone temperature, a species number density, an
integrated flux —  and actively modify the particle population to
drive that quantity toward a target value. They are distinct from
boundary classes (which operate only at patches) and from
measurement classes (which only observe); controllers both observe
and act.

The base interface, ``dsmcController``, is defined under
``src/lagrangian/DSMC/controllers/basic/``. Derived controllers
live under ``controllers/derived/`` and are grouped thematically
into ``temperature/``, ``density/``, and ``others/``.

Implemented controllers
-----------------------

``temperatureController``
`````````````````````````

*Directory:* ``controllers/derived/temperature/temperatureController/``.

Maintains a target translational temperature inside a named cell
zone by periodically rescaling the peculiar velocities of the
parcels it contains. The sampling window over which the kinetic
energy is averaged, the target temperature, and the zone name are
specified in the sub-dictionary.

``maxwellianResamplingController``
``````````````````````````````````

*Directory:*
``controllers/derived/temperature/maxwellianResamplingController/``.

Periodically redraws the full velocity distribution of the parcels
in a zone from a Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution at the target
temperature. Coarser than ``temperatureController`` but occasionally
useful as a hard reset in equilibrium-approach studies.

``densityController``
`````````````````````

*Directory:* ``controllers/derived/density/densityController/``.

Maintains a target per-species number density inside a zone by
inserting parcels when the population is too low and deleting
them when it is too high. Useful in heat-bath studies where a
finite cell volume would otherwise drift in composition.

``gravitationalAccelerationController``
```````````````````````````````````````

*Directory:*
``controllers/derived/others/gravitationalAccelerationController/``.

Applies a user-specified constant acceleration (gravitational or
otherwise) to every parcel in a zone at every DSMC step. The
magnitude and direction are read from a ``acceleration`` vector
key.

Orchestration
-------------

All controllers are instantiated and orchestrated by the
``dsmcControllers`` container (``controllers/basic/dsmcControllers/``),
which is itself constructed by the cloud at initialisation. On
every time-step, each registered controller's ``control()`` entry
point is invoked; internal sampling windows allow a controller to
accumulate statistics over several DSMC steps before it acts.

Use with regression tests
-------------------------

Because controllers modify the particle population, they sit on
the critical path for any deterministic regression test. A test
case that enables a controller inherits the controller's sampling
cadence in the RNG stream, and any change to the controller's
timing or thresholds will perturb the downstream macroscopic
output. The planned regression harness
(:doc:`../guide/regression_tests`) will therefore fix both the
controller parameters and the RNG seed when recording reference
outputs.
