N2 Heat-Bath Relaxation

A zero-dimensional (0-D) isothermal heat-bath relaxation is the cheapest end-to-end validation of the rovibronic relaxation machinery. The gas is initialised at a cold internal temperature while the translational temperature is held high, and the approach to equilibrium is compared to the Landau–Teller analytical solution.

Case layout

run/<case>/
├── 0/              # initial fields
├── constant/       # species, dsmcProperties, rovibronic settings
├── system/         # blockMeshDict, controlDict, decomposeParDict
└── postProcessing/ # populated at runtime

Running

cd run/<case>
blockMesh && dsmcInitialise && dsmcFoamRovibronic

Expected result

  • \(T_v(t)\) relaxes toward \(T_t\) on the Millikan–White timescale.

  • Final state populations match the Boltzmann distribution at \(T_t\) within statistical noise.

  • The \(Z_{\text{fit}}/Z_{\text{input}}\) ratio should sit within \(\approx 1\%\) of unity — confirming the multi-mode LT enforcement is unbiased.

Adding a new example

Create a new .rst in this directory, document the setup and the expected outcome, and add it to the toctree in index.rst.
